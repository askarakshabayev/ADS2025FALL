## Assessment Structure

### Laboratory Works
- Each lab = 1 point

### Quizzes
- 4 quizzes throughout the semester

- Schedule: weeks 4, 8, 12, 16
- One quiz will be a contest format

**Quiz Format** *(Not finalized):*
